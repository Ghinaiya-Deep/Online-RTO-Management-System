Safe Driving Practices for School Buses


School buses are an essential mode of transportation for children, ensuring their safe and reliable commute to and from school. To ensure the safety of young passengers, it is crucial for school bus drivers to adhere to specific safe driving practices.

The RTO in Maharashtra plays a crucial role in promoting school bus safety through various initiatives. They conduct educational programs and training sessions for school bus drivers, focusing on safe driving practices and emergency procedures. Additionally, the RTO conducts regular inspections of school buses to ensure compliance with safety standards.
