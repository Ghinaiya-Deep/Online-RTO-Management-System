Awareness Programs and Activities

Various Awareness program organized for public and how to reduced accident by RTO of Maharashtra.


Check the various Traffic Rules & Presentations associated with Road Safety


  1. Traffic Rules:
    • Traffic Signals: Stop at red, wait at yellow, go at green.
    • Pedestrian Crossings: Use zebra crossings, wait for green.
    • Speed Limits: Follow speed limits to prevent accidents.
    • Seat Belts & Helmets: Wear seat belts in cars, helmets on bikes.

  2. Maharashtra RTO Presentations:
    • RTO conducts presentations on traffic rules.
    • Topics include traffic signs, safe driving, pedestrian and cycling safety.
    • Aims to educate citizens and promote road safety.
